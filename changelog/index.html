Changelog

What we've shipped

Every meaningful change, dated, with the why behind it. Same honesty as the journal, just shorter.

JUNE 2026 New

A new look: the Fastener logo & brand

We rolled out our emblem (a fastener head with four tally bars and a nail as the fifth stroke) across the app and this site. The count, in a mark. It should feel like it belongs on a job site, not in a boardroom.

JUNE 2026 New

Build a job's pull list from a file

Upload what a job needs (a spreadsheet, a CSV, or a PDF) and Nail & Tally turns it into a list you can pull against. Less retyping, fewer missed items before the truck rolls.

JUNE 2026 New

Locations down to the shelf, bin, or truck

A tool isn't just "at the shop" anymore. Sub-locations let you say which shelf, bin, truck, or job site it's on, so finding it is a glance, not a search.

JUNE 2026 Improved

Turn locations on and off

Finished a job site? Mark its location inactive and it drops out of your everyday pickers without losing the history. Bring it back any time.

JUNE 2026 New

A desktop dashboard for managers

Managers and PMs get a big-screen view of the whole inventory (for setup, building pull lists, and managing locations and the item list) while the crew keeps working from their phones in the field.

JUNE 2026 Improved

Tighter sign-in and stronger data separation

We hardened how you sign in and reinforced the walls between companies' data, so each crew's inventory stays private and separate. Security work isn't flashy, but it's the part we won't cut corners on.
